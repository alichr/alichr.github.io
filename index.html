AI/ML/CV Scientist at CSIRO Data61 and Adjunct Faculty Member at the ANU. I specialize in large vision-language models, LLMs, generative AI, knowledge distillation, and incremental learning. I also have extensive AI/ML/CV engineering experience across multiple projects, developing and deploying scalable solutions that bridge cutting-edge research with real-world applications.
